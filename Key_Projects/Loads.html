Total Load Calculations

Project Overview:

Oct 2023 - Oct 2023

➣ A project as an FEA Engineer in association with Blue Hat Solutions Private Limited, Bangalore, India.
➣ I automated load calculations, determining total loads on in-house products from inertial and aerodynamic forces.
➣ Identified the critical load cases, thereby enhancing accuracy and efficiency in the design process.

Role and Responsibilities:

➣ Developed Python code to calculate all possible combinations of loads, incorporating inertial and aerodynamic forces, for in-house products.
➣ Identified and reported the most critical loads, contributing to precise design considerations and ensuring structural integrity.
➣ Collaborated closely with cross-functional teams to ensure comprehensive load analysis and validation.

Results and Outcomes:

➣ Identified Critical Load Cases, contributing to precise design considerations and ensuring structural integrity.
➣ Automated load combination analysis, optimizing efficiency and accuracy in the design process.

Key Skills:

➣ Python(pandas, numpy, matplot), Data Extraction, Data Manipulation, Data Processing, Data Visualization, Loads Calculation
