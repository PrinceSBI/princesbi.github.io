About Me

FEA Engineer

I am Shreyas G S, a graduate holding a Master's degree (MTech) from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in engineering, and I have specialized in areas such as Finite Element Analysis, Machine Learning, and Data Science. In my current role as an FEA Engineer at BlueHat Solutions, I have demonstrated my coding proficiency by developing Python scripts to handle large datasets and automate various aspects of the design process. This includes extracting aerodynamic data, conducting exploratory data analysis, and implementing machine learning and Fast Fourier Transform techniques for Flight Data Analysis.

In addition to my technical skills, my leadership capabilities were refined during my time with Team SHUNYA at IIT Bombay. As a Senior Design Engineer, I took charge of the Automation division and Electrical Subsystems for Project Vivaan, an initiative focused on constructing affordable pre-fabricated net-zero solar-powered houses. Notably, our team secured the 2nd place in the prestigious "Solar Decathlon" organized by the US Department of Energy. My responsibilities included leading automation efforts, collaborating on the integration of Electrical and Mechanical Subsystems, and contributing significantly to the design, documentation, and testing of automation systems.

This unique combination of technical expertise and leadership experience positions me as a versatile professional ready to contribute to innovative projects and tackle new challenges.
